About EcoLearn Academy

We are an environmental education nonprofit that builds community knowledge and hands-on skills — from school programs to citizen science.

Our Mission

To make environmental literacy accessible. We run teacher trainings, youth clubs, public workshops and real-world restoration projects.

Featured Courses

Short, practical and community-focused modules — suitable for teachers, teens and adults.
